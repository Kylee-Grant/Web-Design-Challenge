City Latitude vs. Cloudiness

This visualization displays the cloudiness (%) for all cities in the dataset, organized by latitude. There is not an immediately discernible trend, and the correlation reflected here is the weakest of all those currently featured on this site. In a separate linear regression with a similar dataset, no statistically significant relationship was found between cloudiness and latitude.
