What's the weather like as you approach the equator? How could you prove your answer?

Introduction

In this project, the students of Northwestern University's Data Science and Visualization Boot Camp were challenged to answer the questions above. Using a simple Python library and OpenWeatherMap, more than 500 cities were selected via a random sample to create a representative model of weather across the world. These cities were used to explore the relationship between latitude and various aspects of weather: temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed.


Visualizations

These visualizations reflect the aforementioned relationships between latitute and aspects of weather. Learn more by clicking directly on these visualizations or navigate using the menu bar.
